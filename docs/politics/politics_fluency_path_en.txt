Privacy Policy

Fluency Path is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Information we collect

• Name and email address

• Information about app behavior (via tools like Google Analytics or Firebase)

2. How we use your information

• To personalize your experience and show your progress
• To send you learning-related notifications (optional)
• To manage payments and in-app purchases (when available)

3. Storage and security

Your data is securely stored on our servers and protected against unauthorized access. Encryption and access control measures are implemented.

4. Data sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, unless required by law or to complete a transaction authorized by you.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, you can contact us via email.
